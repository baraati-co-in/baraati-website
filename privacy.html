Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026  ·  Effective: 22 May 2026

Baraati ("we", "our", or "us") is a private wedding companion platform operated by Baraati Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Baraati mobile application or website at baraati.co.in.

By using Baraati, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. This policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name and email address (required to create an account)
  • Mobile number (used for host contact and travel coordination)
  • Profile photograph (optional, uploaded by you)

Wedding & Guest Data

  • Wedding details entered by hosts: couple names, date, venue, hashtag
  • Guest information: arrival dates, travel mode, room assignments
  • Government-issued ID documents uploaded by guests for hotel check-in purposes

Content You Share

  • Photos and videos posted to the wedding feed or I-Spy challenges
  • Reactions and captions attached to posts

Device & Usage Data

  • Push notification tokens (to deliver wedding updates)
  • Basic device information (OS version, app version) for debugging
  • Crash reports and error logs (no personally identifiable content)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core wedding app experience — schedule, guest list, feed, I-Spy
  • To send push notifications authorised by the wedding host
  • To enable hosts to coordinate guest travel, room assignments, and ID collection
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To respond to support requests sent to us directly

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to third parties.

3. Government ID Documents

Government IDs uploaded by guests are stored securely in encrypted cloud storage (Cloudflare R2). Access is restricted to the wedding host for the sole purpose of hotel or venue check-in coordination. IDs are not shared with any other party. Guests may request deletion of their ID at any time by contacting us or the host.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following trusted service providers, bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (data stored in AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai)
  • Cloudflare R2 — media and document storage
  • Expo / Apple / Google — push notification delivery

We do not share your data with wedding vendors, advertisers, or any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Wedding data (schedule, guest list, feed) is retained for 12 months after the wedding date, then automatically deleted unless the host requests an extension
  • Government ID documents are deleted within 30 days after the wedding date
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to erasure")
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Raise a grievance with us regarding data handling

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@baraati.co.in. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS), row-level security on our database, and access controls limiting who can view sensitive data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take every reasonable measure to protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

Baraati is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify users via the app or email if the changes are material.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026  ·  Effective: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Baraati LLP ("Baraati", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use the Baraati mobile application and related websites at baraati.co.in, including the post-wedding album (the "Service").

Baraati is a private, invite-only platform for organising and participating in weddings. Within each wedding, some users are Hosts (the couple, family, or planners who administer that wedding) and others are Guests. A single account may be a Host in one wedding and a Guest in another.

By using Baraati, you agree to this Policy. It is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), and, where applicable, addresses the EU/UK GDPR. We do not collect your device's GPS location, do not use advertising identifiers, and do not run third-party advertising or behavioural-tracking analytics.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Profile

  • First and last name, email address, and mobile number
  • City and optional date of birth
  • Optional profile photograph

Wedding Information (entered by Hosts)

  • Couple/bride/groom names, wedding hashtag, dates and ceremonies
  • Venue names, addresses, map links and coordinates, and venue contacts
  • Expected guest count and a hero/couple photograph
  • Room/accommodation allocations for guests

Travel & Accommodation (provided by Guests, optional)

  • Arrival and departure dates and times
  • Mode of travel, city of origin, and flight/train number

Content You Share

  • Photos and videos posted to the wedding feed, Behind-the-Scenes, or I-Spy
  • Captions, reactions, "Know the Couple" stories, and game answers
  • Your RSVP status and whether you wish to appear as a contact to other guests

Government ID Documents (optional)

  • A government-issued ID, uploaded only if a Host requests it for hotel/venue check-in (see Section 3)

Authentication, Device & Usage Data

  • Your email address and the one-time code used to sign you in (we use one-time-code login only — no passwords or social logins)
  • Push notification tokens (to deliver wedding updates)
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Crash and error reports for stability and debugging
  • IP address and device/user-agent for government-ID access events

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and operate your account and verify it via one-time code
  • To run weddings — schedule, venues, guest list, RSVPs, feed, games and leaderboards
  • To deliver in-app and push notifications about wedding activity
  • To let Hosts coordinate travel, rooms, and (where requested and consented) ID-based check-in
  • To secure the Service, prevent abuse, enforce limits, and detect compromised devices
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability, and to respond to support requests

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell it.

3. Government ID Documents

The Service lets a Guest upload a government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, voter ID, driving licence, or other) only when a Host has requested it, for the sole purpose of hotel/venue check-in coordination. This is always optional and consent-based.

Each document is encrypted with AES-256-GCM envelope encryption and stored in private, access-controlled storage (Cloudflare R2). Documents are never served as public links — when an authorised person views one, it is decrypted and streamed for that single request. Access is restricted to you and the Hosts of that specific wedding, and every access is logged with the user, time, IP address, and device. ID features are disabled on devices detected as rooted/jailbroken, and accessing them requires recent re-authentication.

Government IDs are automatically and permanently deleted 3 days after the last day of the wedding they were uploaded for. You may also delete yours sooner at any time in the app or by contacting us.

4. Photos, Videos & User Content

Content you post is shared with members of that wedding. Feed photos, videos, avatars, and hero images are stored on content-delivery storage and served via unguessable links; these files are not individually access-controlled at the storage layer, so anyone with a link can view that file. Where a Host publishes a post-wedding album, selected content becomes viewable by people the Host shares it with. You are responsible for content you post, including having permission to post images of other people, and being especially mindful of photos that include children.

5. Data Sharing & Service Providers

We share data only within a wedding (with that wedding's Hosts and Guests per role — government IDs and travel/room details only with that wedding's Hosts), for legal reasons, in a business transfer, and with the following service providers:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and serverless functions
  • Cloudflare — encrypted storage of media and IDs (R2), and hosting of the post-wedding album (Workers)
  • Resend — delivery of one-time login codes by email
  • Expo — push notification delivery and app updates
  • Sentry — crash and error diagnostics
  • Razorpay — payment processing, only if and when paid plans are processed

We do not share your data with wedding vendors, advertisers, or any other third parties, and we never sell it.

6. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data is retained while your account exists, then deleted on account deletion
  • Wedding content is retained for the life of the wedding space; a published album persists for up to 3 years (or longer if its validity is extended/renewed), then expires
  • Government ID documents are automatically and permanently deleted 3 days after the wedding's last day, or sooner on request
  • Security and access-audit logs are retained for a limited period for legal compliance
  • Incomplete/abandoned sign-ups are cleaned up automatically

7. Your Rights

Under applicable law (the DPDP Act and, where relevant, the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to erasure")
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. for government-ID storage or notifications) at any time
  • Nominate another person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity
  • Raise a grievance with our Grievance Officer

To exercise these rights, email us at support@baraati.co.in. We will respond within 30 days. You can delete your account at any time in the app (Profile → Delete Account) or via baraati.co.in/delete-account.

8. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS for all traffic with certificate pinning in the app, Row-Level Security isolating each wedding's data, encrypted device session storage, AES-256-GCM envelope encryption for government IDs with audited and streamed-only access, disabling of sensitive features on compromised devices, re-authentication gating for ID access, and rate limiting on sensitive operations. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we take every reasonable measure to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly allow individuals under 18 to create accounts or knowingly collect their personal data, and we do not use any data for tracking or targeted advertising. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. International Transfers

We are based in India and primarily store data for the Indian region. Some service providers may process data on servers outside India; where we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards and only transfer to jurisdictions permitted under applicable Indian law. For EU/UK users, transfers use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date and notify users via the app or email if the changes are material.

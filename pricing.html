Questions

Pricing, answered

Is it really one payment per wedding?

Yes. Standard and Premium are a single, one-time payment that covers your whole wedding — every day, every ceremony, and unlimited guests. There's no monthly subscription.

Do guests have to pay anything?

Never. Guests join with an invite code and use everything — RSVP, photo feed, games, travel details — completely free. Only the host chooses a plan.

What's the wedding album?

With Premium, when your celebrations wrap up you can turn all the photos, I-Spy captures and games into a private album with its own website (like yourwedding.baraati.co.in). It stays live for 3 years, after which you can keep it for ₹510/year.

Can I start free and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Begin on the free plan to set things up, then upgrade to Standard or Premium anytime — your wedding and everything in it carries over.

How do I pay right now?

Secure online checkout is on the way. In the meantime, just message us on WhatsApp and we'll get your wedding set up on the plan you want.

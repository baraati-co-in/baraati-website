Delete your Baraati account

You can permanently delete your Baraati account and personal data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open the Baraati app and sign in.
  2. Go to your Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Your account and data are erased immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email us from your registered email address with the subject "Delete my account". We will verify and process the request within 30 days.

Email support@baraati.co.in

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, mobile, city, date of birth, profile photo)
  • Photos and videos you uploaded (feed, Behind-the-Scenes, I-Spy)
  • Any government ID documents you uploaded
  • Your RSVPs, travel/accommodation details, and game activity
  • Your push-notification tokens and login identity

What may be retained

  • Limited security and access-audit logs we are required to keep for legal and safety reasons, retained only for a limited period.
  • Content you contributed that a wedding host has independently exported or published (e.g. in a post-wedding album) may remain outside our control.

If you are the owner/host of a wedding, transfer ownership to a co-host before deleting: open the wedding → Admin → Guests, make a co-host an admin, then tap Make Owner. You can delete your account once you no longer own any wedding.

Questions about deletion or your data? Contact support@baraati.co.in. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data retention.

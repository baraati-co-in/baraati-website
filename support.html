Contact us

Questions, feedback, or trouble with the app? Email us and a real person will get back to you, usually within one business day.

  • Email: support@baraati.co.in

Report content or a user

Baraati has zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behaviour. In the app, tap the "•••" menu on any feed post, I‑Spy capture, or profile to:

  • Report it — the content is hidden from you immediately and the wedding's hosts are notified. We review reports and remove violating content within 24 hours.
  • Block the person — you'll no longer see anything they post. Manage blocked accounts under Profile → Blocked Accounts.

You can also report content or safety concerns directly to support@baraati.co.in and we'll act on it promptly.

Manage your account

  • Delete your account — in the app under Profile → Delete Account, or see our account deletion page.
  • Government ID documents — uploads are optional, encrypted, shared only with your wedding's hosts, and auto‑deleted 3 days after the wedding. You can delete yours anytime in the app.
  • Notifications — control push notifications from your device settings.

Common questions

How do I join a wedding?

Open the invite link your host shared, or enter the invite code on the Join screen after signing in.

I'm not receiving my one‑time code

Check spam/filters for email codes, confirm your number for SMS, wait for the 60‑second cooldown, then request another. If it persists, email us.

How do I leave a wedding or remove a guest?

Guests can leave from their profile; hosts can remove guests from the Guests tab.

Everything a wedding needs,
in one private app.

From the first invitation to the album that outlives the week — here is everything Baraati does for your guests and for the family running the show.

Invitations & RSVP

An invitation they can feel. RSVPs you can count on.

Every guest opens a full-screen animated invitation — your photo in a parallax hero, your names in poster type, every ceremony laid out day by day. To say yes, they slide: one satisfying gesture, a burst of petals, and you get a push the moment it happens.

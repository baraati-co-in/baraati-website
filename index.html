What is Baraati

One beautiful space
for your entire wedding

Indian weddings are multi-day, multi-event celebrations with hundreds of guests travelling from across the country. Baraati replaces scattered WhatsApp groups, PDFs, and phone calls with a single private app — personalised for your wedding.

🎨

Themed Experience

Choose from 6 distinct visual themes. Every guest sees your wedding's unique personality the moment they open the app.

🔐

Private by Design

Guests join only via your invite code. No public listings, no strangers — just your people.

📱

Everything in One Place

Schedule, venue, guest list, photos, travel info, and notifications — all inside one app.

✨

Live & Real-time

Updates happen instantly. Change a venue, send a notification, share a photo — guests see it right away.

Questions

The fine print.

How do guests join the wedding? ＋

Guests download the Baraati app and enter the 6-character invite code the host shares with them. They pick their side (Bride's, Groom's, or Event Team) and they're in — no lengthy sign-up required.

Is my wedding private? ＋

Yes. Every wedding is invitation-only — accessible only via your unique invite code. Photos, games, and the social feed all live inside your wedding's private space. No public listings, no strangers.

What games are available? ＋

Baraati includes three built-in games: Word Search (find hidden wedding words in a 15×15 grid), Predictions (vote on questions about the couple), and Who's Most Likely? (rapid-fire 15-second bride-or-groom questions). All games feed into a shared leaderboard.

What is I-Spy? ＋

I-Spy is a photo challenge where guests capture specific wedding moments — with the Bride, with the Groom, on the dance floor, candid emotions, and more. Each guest can upload up to 10 photos per moment, and everyone can react to submissions.

Can the host collect guest travel & ID details? ＋

Yes. Guests can submit their travel itinerary (arrival/departure date, time, mode, and confirmation number), and hosts can export it as a CSV. Guests can also upload government-issued ID documents (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License) for hotel check-in — stored securely and viewable only by the host.
